# Case 1 Sharepoint Developer Senior

## Requirement

You have to implement a vehicle rental solution which allows internal users to rent vehicles from the company's fleet. A form is used, which the employee has to fill out when renting a vehicle. The company has multiple locations around the world.

Your task is to implement this application into the M365/Azure Cloud for internal use of the employees.

## Questions

1. How do you structure the solution and which components do you choose and why? Feel free to outline different solution options at a high level
2. How would you solve the time zone challenge regarding different locations of the company?
3. What technologies and frameworks would you use in your components and why (eg. form validation)?
4. How do you solve authentication/authorization and role-based access, and which roles does the solution need? What do you need to configure for this in entra id?
5. Briefly explain how the authentication/authorization flows you plan to adopt work in Azure/M365.
6. What data storage would you use? You can mention a couple of options - also mention their advantages/disadvantages.
