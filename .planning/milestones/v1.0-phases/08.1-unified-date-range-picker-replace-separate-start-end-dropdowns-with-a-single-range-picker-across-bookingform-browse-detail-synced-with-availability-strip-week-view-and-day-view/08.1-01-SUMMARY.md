## Decisions Made
- Used Calendar key prop (concatenation of start/end timestamps) to force full re-mount when range changes, ensuring customDayCellRef fires for all cells -- simplest reliable approach per research Pitfall 1
- Replaced imperative handleFormDateChange callback with reactive useEffect on range.startDate for strip position sync -- cleaner unidirectional data flow
- Both hour dropdowns now use equal-width `.formField` class since DatePickers are gone (was previously `.formFieldNarrow` for the hour dropdown next to wider DatePicker)
- Added range/onRangeChange as optional props to AvailabilityStrip and AvailabilityTimeline now so VehicleDetail JSX is fully wired; Plans 02/03 will make them required
